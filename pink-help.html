Frequently Asked Questions
How do I earn coins?
You can earn coins by watching videos, completing daily surveys, referring friends, and claiming daily rewards. Each activity gives a different amount of coins. Video watching gives 5-50 coins per video, surveys give 100-500 coins, and referrals give you 10% of your friend's earnings.
How do I withdraw my earnings?
Go to your dashboard and tap "Withdraw". You need a minimum balance of 5,000 coins and a verified bank account. Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours. Make sure your bank details are correct to avoid delays.
What is the minimum withdrawal amount?
The minimum withdrawal is 5,000 coins (equivalent to 2,500 Naira for Nigerian users). This amount varies by country based on local conversion rates. Check your dashboard for your country's specific rate.
How does the referral system work?
Share your referral code or link with friends. When they sign up and start earning, you get a 10% bonus on their earnings for life. The more they earn, the more you earn. You can find your referral code in the Referral section of your dashboard.
Why was my withdrawal rejected?
Withdrawals may be rejected if your account is not verified, you haven't met the minimum threshold, your bank details are incorrect, or there are suspicious activities detected. Contact support for help resolving the issue.
Can I use Pink App outside Africa?
Pink App is currently available for users in African countries only. We are working to expand to other regions soon. Your account will be locked if accessed from outside supported countries.
How do I verify my account?
After signing up, check your email for a verification link. Click it to verify your account. You can also resend the verification email from your profile page. Verified accounts have higher withdrawal limits.
How do I change my country?
Go to Profile > Edit Profile to change your country. Note that changing your country will update your coin conversion rate. You can only change your country once every 30 days.
What is the daily reward streak?
The daily reward streak gives you bonus coins for consecutive daily check-ins. Day 1 = 10 coins, Day 2 = 20 coins, up to Day 7 = 100 coins. If you miss a day, the streak resets to Day 1. Keep your streak alive for maximum rewards!
Is Pink App free to use?
Yes! Pink App is completely free to download and use. You never need to pay anything to earn coins. We make money from advertisers, and we share that revenue with you.
