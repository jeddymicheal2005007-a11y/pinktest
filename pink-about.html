What is Pink App?

Pink App is a social earning platform built for Africans. We partner with content creators and advertisers to bring you curated video content, surveys, and daily tasks.

Every video you watch, every survey you complete, and every friend you refer earns you coins that convert directly to real cash in your local currency.

Founded in 2024, Pink App has already paid out millions to thousands of users across Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, and 50+ African countries.
